High quality statistics, analysis and advice to help Britain make better decisions

At the Office for National Statistics, we are

  • filling the gaps in economic statistics highlighted in the Bean Review, improving statistics on trade, productivity, prices and the flow of funds.
  • informing public policy by improving population and migration estimates using administrative data, delivering the 2021 census, and improving statistics on societal issues such as crime, health and inequalities.
  • enhancing our data capability through innovative use of modern technology, exploring new possibiities in our Data Science Campus, and growing a diverse and high performing workforce.

The 2021 Census

To prepare for the 2021 Census, we are

  • holding a rehearsal in autumn 2019 in four selected local authorities
  • developing the questions to be included in the 2021 Census questionnaire
  • holding regional roadshows to share news and information about the census
